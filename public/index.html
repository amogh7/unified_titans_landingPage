Foundation Unified Titans Table tennis Academy

2009:Laid foundation by opening first academy in Gurgaon at Sector 4 by the founder-Mr. Prashant Yadav with vision of promoting the game of table tennis with the right coaching and guidance to the talented students. it has over 150 players actively getting professional training as of today

2012: co-founder -Mr Ankit Gupta laid roots of Unified Titans Table Tennis Academy in faridabad which remains the second biggest center till date with over 125 Students taking coaching at professional scale

Unified Titans Table tennis Academy players Continue to attend some of Indias most prestigious camps and bring laurels in the national laevel tournaments across the country
